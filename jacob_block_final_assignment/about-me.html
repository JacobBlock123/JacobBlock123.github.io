    Hello there! I'm Jacob Block, the picutre above shows me in 2018 with my sister and my dad, I grew up and continue to live in Winnipeg Manitoba. I love technology and have for a long time and made this blog to share my love for tech with others who share this passion. I also have a lot of other interests though. I love to play the guitar, I love playing badminton, I enjoy card games and board games, and I love the outdoors and camping.

    I always loved being active (as most kids do). I was always running around and barely ever stopped moving. I played all sort of sports such as soccer, volleyball, flag football and basketball. But I also loved video games. When I was quite young, probably around when I was 7 or 8, I got a Wii. This sparked my love for video games. After the Wii I got an Xbox One, then I got a PS4, and finally I built my own PC. Building my own PC is the main thing that sparked my interest in tech. I enjoyed the experience and proccess of building the PC so much that I built many more after. This is what really got me into computer hardware, and naturally I also began to become interested in the software side of computers. I learned a lot a out software and hardware through a program called PTEC, which is an advanced technology program that sent me to MITT in grade 11. Since the start of PTEC in grade 9 and going to MITT in grade 11 I have continued to learn about tech and my passion for it has only grown.
